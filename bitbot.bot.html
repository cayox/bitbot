Parameters

config (str or dict[str,any]) – the configuration that the bot should use

run()¶

Method to start the Bot. Runs in an endless loop and alternates between buying and selling, based on the signal generated by the strategy used. Always executes market orders. Sleeps for the update_interval Seconds at the end of every loop iteration.

Saves buying time, buying proceeds and order direction in an attribute called history
